A sale is classified as a Complex Order for managing as a Programme if any of the following are true:
It includes CIS, EDS, or a combination of products being delivered as an Integrated System (INT) solution.
It includes any non-standard product.
Is a variation order to an existing project
It includes a virtual server
It includes a standard product and
The contract will require significant coordination, risk or effort to deliver, install and maintain through service and support.
The contract will require significant resource coordination (such as from PID or Operations) to deliver and meet customer expectations.
